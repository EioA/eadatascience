Microarray for cancer detection

Microarray data has been instrumental in advancing our understanding of gene expression, disease mechanisms, drug discovery, and personalized medicine.Microarray data refers to a type of high-throughput biological data generated from microarray technology. Microarrays are tools used in molecular biology and genomics to simultaneously measure the expression levels of thousands of genes or other biological molecules in a biological sample. Learn more

Cancer Diagnosis From Mammogram Images

Cancer diagnosis from mammogram images is a critical application of medical imaging and radiology in the field of breast cancer detection. Mammography is a standard and widely used screening tool for breast cancer.The development and use of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning techniques for the automated interpretation of mammograms hold promise for improving breast cancer detection rates and reducing false positives and false negatives.
